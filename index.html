Our Process

The Keryx System™

Five phases. Every client. No shortcuts. Each letter of KERYX stands for a distinct phase of the system — not a mnemonic, but a map from first contact to compounding revenue.

K Know

Phase 01

Know Your Audience

We audit your full account, map your customer behaviour, and pinpoint exactly where revenue is leaking before we touch a single tool.

  • Full account audit
  • Deliverability check
  • Customer journey mapping
  • Revenue gap analysis
E Engineer

Phase 02

Engineer the Strategy

Flow logic, segmentation structure, send cadence, brand voice — the entire architecture is mapped and approved before a single email is built.

  • Flow map & blueprint
  • Segmentation strategy
  • Brand voice framework
  • Campaign calendar
R Reach

Phase 03

Reach the Inbox

Every core automation goes live — copy, design, logic, and timing — built to land in the primary inbox and convert from day one.

  • Welcome series
  • Abandoned checkout
  • Abandoned browse
  • Post-purchase & win-back

Phase 04

Yield the Data

Once live, we dissect the data — A/B tests, send-time analysis, revenue attribution per flow. Every decision is tied to a real number.

  • Subject line A/B testing
  • Send time optimisation
  • Revenue attribution per flow
  • List health monitoring
X Expand

Phase 05

Expand the Revenue

With the foundation performing, we go deeper. VIP sequences, advanced personalisation, list growth. The longer the Keryx System runs, the more it compounds.

  • VIP & loyalty sequences
